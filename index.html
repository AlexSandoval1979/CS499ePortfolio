In practice, databases are organized collections of structured data, managed by database management systems (DBMS) (What Is a Database?, 2023). Databases employ tables with rows and columns to optimize processing and querying (What Is a Database?, 2023). Data access, management, modification, update, and organization are seamless in databases, with SQL being the widely used language for data writing and querying (What Is a Database?, 2023).
